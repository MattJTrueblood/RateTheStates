What is this?

This is a website to poll public opinions of ratings for the 50 US States. Here, you can create a personal rating map. You can submit the map to a central server, where at the end the average rating for each state will be compiled together into an overall rating map.

Why isn't DC included in the counter for remaining states?

DC is not a state. You can rate it all you want, but it will not be included in the final submission.
